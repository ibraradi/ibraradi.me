I've spent the last few years building security platforms and breaking into things on purpose — and most of what I learn ends up scattered across notes, DMs, and CTF write-ups nobody else reads. This blog is where I'll collect the parts worth keeping.

## What I'll write about

- **Attack Surface Management** — lessons from building a distributed scanning engine that maps thousands of assets fast.
- **Threat intelligence** — parsing messy data at scale (billions of leaked records) without drowning.
- **Offensive security** — bug bounty findings, pentest patterns, and CTF write-ups across web, OSINT, and mobile.
- **DevSecOps** — keeping pipelines fast *and* safe.

## Why bother

Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain how an exploit chain works or why a detection fires, I probably don't understand it as well as I think. Publishing keeps me honest.
